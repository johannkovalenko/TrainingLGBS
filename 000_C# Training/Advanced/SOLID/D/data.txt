10-week training-plan for Jarek C#:

5 Levels á 2 Weeks
	Week 1: 100% training (40 hours) (no other tasks = no excuses)
		20 exercises (all from Marcin)
		two case studies (one from Marcin, one from Johann)
	Week 2: Help team with real RPA tasks to consolidate knowledge
        in case of lack of tasks (20 additional exercises)

Criteria to finish training
    90% solved exercises (including possible additional exercises)
    90% solved case studies
    One final test after 10 weeks (minimum 60% required to pass)
